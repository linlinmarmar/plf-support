Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Plf ("the app") is a transit companion app for London, showing live departure information for tube stations, bus stops, and National Rail stations.

What data Plf collects

Plf does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app does not require an account, login, or registration. There are no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs in the app. No crash reporting tools are integrated.

Data stored on your device

When you save a station or bus stop view, your preferences (station name, selected lines, and destination filters) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

You can delete your saved views at any time from within the app.

Network requests

The app makes network requests to the following third-party APIs to fetch live departure data:

  • Transport for London (TfL) Unified API — for tube and bus departure predictions and line status information
  • National Rail Darwin API — for National Rail departure predictions

These requests contain the station or stop ID being queried and an API key registered to the developer. They do not contain any information that identifies you personally. As with any internet request, your device's IP address is transmitted to these servers as part of the network connection. The developer does not have access to these server logs.

For information about how TfL handles data, see: tfl.gov.uk/corporate/privacy-and-cookies

For information about how National Rail handles data, see: nationalrail.co.uk/privacy-policy

Third-party services

Plf does not share any data with third parties for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose. The app does not use any third-party SDKs.

Children's privacy

Plf does not knowingly collect any data from children. The app does not require any personal information to use.

Data attribution

Departure data displayed in the app is provided by Transport for London and National Rail Enquiries. The app displays this data under the terms of the TfL Transport Data Service and the National Rail open data licence.
